創作者 / Artists Phase 01 — Founding Artists

精選藝術家。
Feature Artists. Our Artists.

來自猶太世界與華人世界的創作者，在此相遇。每一位都是創始成員。 Creators from the Jewish and Chinese worlds, meeting here. Each one a founding member.

Ann Chen

台北，台灣 Taipei, Taiwan

視覺藝術Visual Art 字體與書法Typography & Calligraphy 攝影Photography

以色列與台灣之間的文化移動，是我創作的起點。我以攝影與設計作為語言，關注文字、信仰、城市記憶與人之間的情感距離。作品常結合中文與希伯來文，透過影像、排版與敘事，探索身份、觀看方式與文化共存的可能。 The cultural movement between Israel and Taiwan is the starting point of my work. Using photography and design as language, I explore the emotional distance between text, faith, urban memory, and people. My work often combines Chinese and Hebrew, using image, typography, and narrative to explore identity and cultural coexistence.
