02 — 申請流程 02 — How to Apply

三步驟加入。 Three steps to join.

Step 01

填寫申請表 Fill Out the Application

說明你的文化背景、創作媒介，以及你希望在這個平台上做什麼。沒有標準答案。 Share your cultural background, creative medium, and what you hope to do here. There are no right answers.

Step 02

審核與對話 Review & Conversation

我們閱讀每一份申請。通過初選後，安排一次對話——我們想認識你這個人。 We read every application. If selected, we'll arrange a conversation — we want to know you as a person.

Step 03

成為創始成員 Become a Founding Member

加入第一個 20 人核心圈，共同定義 Manbrew Bridge 的結構、文化與優先項目。 Join the first circle of 20, and help define the structure, culture, and priorities of Manbrew Bridge.
