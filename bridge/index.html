02 — 平台理念 02 — Why Bridge

兩個古老文明，
一個授權平台。 Two ancient cultures.
One licensing platform.

猶太文化與華人文化都擁有深厚的視覺語言傳統——從希伯來書法到漢字書寫，從流散美學到身份敘事。這兩種文明的相遇，不只是文化交流，更是商業機會。

Manbrew Bridge 是一個策展型數位藝術授權平台。我們篩選來自這兩個文化脈絡的藝術家，讓設計師、品牌、出版商與收藏者能夠授權使用真正具有文化深度的數位藝術作品。

每一件作品都帶著故事。授權之後，這個故事延伸到你的創作裡。

Jewish and Chinese cultures share a deep tradition of visual language — from Hebrew calligraphy to Chinese characters, from diaspora aesthetics to narratives of faith and identity. The intersection of these two civilizations creates a unique creative and commercial opportunity.

Manbrew Bridge is a curated digital art licensing platform. We select artists from both cultural contexts so designers, brands, publishers, and collectors can license works with genuine cultural depth.

Every artwork carries a story. When licensed, that story becomes part of your creative work.

04 — 藝術家分潤 04 — For Artists

上架作品，賺取被動收入。 List your work.
Earn passive income.

  • ◈
    80% 授權收益歸你 80% of every license goes to you

    每次授權購買，你獲得 80%。平台只收取 20% 作為維運費用。 Every time your work is licensed, you receive 80%. We keep 20% to run the platform.

  • ✦
    版權永遠歸屬於你 You keep full copyright

    授權不等於賣斷。你的作品永遠屬於你，你可以隨時下架或修改。 Licensing is not a sale. Your artwork remains yours — you can remove or update it at any time.

  • ⬡
    全球受眾，文化精準 Global audience, cultural precision

    我們的收藏者與買家來自設計、出版、品牌等領域，他們正在尋找具有文化深度的作品。 Our buyers are designers, publishers, and brands actively looking for culturally rooted artworks they can't find elsewhere.

  • ▸
    策展式曝光，不是演算法 Curated exposure, not algorithm-driven

    你的作品以完整品質呈現，而非在無盡滾動中消失。每件作品都被認真對待。 Your work is presented with full context, not lost in an infinite scroll. Every piece is treated with intention.
