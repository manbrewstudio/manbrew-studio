授權說明Licensing Guide

你購買的是什麼？
授權，不是版權。 What you're buying.
A license, not ownership.

在 Manbrew Bridge 購買藝術作品，你取得的是數位檔案的使用授權——用於特定用途的合法使用權。藝術家的版權始終由其本人持有。

When you purchase artwork on Manbrew Bridge, you're acquiring a license — the legal right to use a digital file for specific purposes. The artist's copyright always remains with them.

常見問題Frequently Asked Questions

授權購買前，先看這裡。 Read this before you buy.

我購買的是什麼？ What exactly am I purchasing?

你購買的是高解析度數位檔案的使用授權，用於你所選擇的授權類型允許的用途。你不持有作品版權，也不能聲稱此作品為你的創作。 You're purchasing a license to use a high-resolution digital file for the purposes permitted under your chosen license type. You do not own the copyright and cannot claim the work as your own creation.

購買後如何取得檔案？ How do I receive the file after purchase?

目前平台採人工授權流程：購買後，我們將在 1–2 個工作天內透過 email 發送高解析度數位檔案及授權確認書。 Currently the platform uses a manual licensing process. After purchase, we'll send you the high-resolution digital file and license confirmation via email within 1–2 business days.

我可以列印這件作品嗎？ Can I print the artwork?

個人授權允許列印最多 5 份，用於家庭展示或個人用途。商業授權允許用於出版、廣告及商品（最多 500 件）。 Personal license permits printing up to 5 copies for home display or personal use. Commercial license permits use in publications, advertising, and merchandise (up to 500 units).

可以用於商業品牌或廣告嗎？ Can I use this for commercial branding or advertising?

需要購買商業授權。個人授權不涵蓋任何商業用途。 You need to purchase a Commercial License. Personal License does not cover any commercial use.

藝術家保有作品版權嗎？ Does the artist keep copyright?

是的。所有作品版權歸屬於藝術家本人。平台取得的是數位分發授權，購買者取得的是使用授權。沒有任何一方取得版權所有權。 Yes. All artwork copyright remains with the artist. The platform holds digital distribution rights; buyers hold usage rights. Neither party holds copyright ownership.

如果我不滿意，可以退款嗎？ Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

由於數位授權的性質，一旦數位檔案已交付，原則上無法退款。如果你在收到檔案前取消購買，或收到的檔案有技術問題（如損毀、解析度不符），請聯繫我們，我們將個案處理。 Due to the nature of digital licensing, refunds are generally not available once the digital file has been delivered. If you cancel before receiving the file, or if the file has technical issues (corruption, resolution mismatch), please contact us and we'll handle it on a case-by-case basis.

我可以將授權轉讓給客戶嗎？ Can I transfer the license to my client?

不可以。授權是個人或企業不可轉讓的使用權。如果你是設計師或代理商，希望為客戶取得授權，請以客戶名義購買，或聯繫我們洽談代理授權方案。 No. Licenses are non-transferable personal or business use rights. If you're a designer or agency purchasing on behalf of a client, purchase in the client's name, or contact us to discuss agency licensing arrangements.

有沒有辦法取得獨家授權？ Is exclusive licensing available?

標準授權均為非獨家授權。如需獨家授權（即同一時期只有你可以使用此作品），請聯繫我們：hello@manbrew-studio.com。獨家授權將與藝術家直接協商，費用依需求而定。 Standard licenses are all non-exclusive. If you need exclusive rights (meaning only you can use the work during a given period), contact us at hello@manbrew-studio.com. Exclusive licensing is negotiated directly with the artist and priced accordingly.
