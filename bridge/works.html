時間之外Beyond Time

Ann Chen · 台北，台灣Taipei, Taiwan

這張作品拍攝於耶路撒冷老城。畫面中的人物彷彿停留在歷史與當下之間，時間不再是線性的流動，而是一種被信仰與記憶包覆的存在。 Shot in the Old City of Jerusalem. The figure seems suspended between history and the present — time no longer linear, but held within faith and memory.
