平台使命Platform Mission

為什麼是猶太與華人藝術家？ Why Chinese &
Jewish artists?

猶太文化與華人文化擁有世界上最深厚的文字、書法與視覺藝術傳統。這兩種文明的相遇，創造出無法在任何一般素材庫找到的視覺語言。

Manbrew Bridge 不是大眾市集，而是一個高度策展的授權平台。我們只接受具有文化深度的創作者，因此每一件作品都有其不可取代的獨特性。

Jewish and Chinese cultures carry two of the world's deepest traditions in script, calligraphy, and visual art. The intersection of these two civilizations creates a visual language that cannot be found in any generic stock library.

Manbrew Bridge is not a mass marketplace — it is a highly curated licensing platform. We only accept creators with genuine cultural depth, which is why every work here is irreplaceable.

稀缺性

這裡的作品無法被 AI 完整複製，無法在 Getty 找到。文化深度就是護城河。 These works cannot be fully replicated by AI or found on Getty. Cultural depth is the moat.

策展

每位藝術家都經過人工審核。我們對作品質量負責，而非靠演算法決定曝光。 Every artist is reviewed by a human. We are editorially responsible for quality, not algorithmically driven.

連結

第一批 20 位創始藝術家將共同塑造平台的文化方向與策展標準。 The first 20 founding artists will co-shape the platform's cultural direction and curatorial standards.

申請流程How It Works

藝術家常見問題Artist FAQ

你可能想知道的。 Questions you might have.

我沒有猶太或華人文化背景，可以申請嗎？ I don't have a Jewish or Chinese background — can I still apply?

目前平台限定猶太或華人文化背景的創作者。如果你的作品深度受到這兩種文化影響，歡迎在申請表中說明，我們將個案評估。 Currently the platform is limited to creators with Jewish or Chinese cultural backgrounds. If your work is deeply influenced by either culture, please explain in the application — we evaluate on a case-by-case basis.

上架後多久才會有買家？ How long until buyers find my work?

平台目前處於初期建設階段。我們會在買家推廣上持續努力，但無法保證特定時間內有銷售。建議把這個平台看作長期的被動收入來源，而非即時收益。 The platform is currently in early development. We'll invest in buyer outreach, but cannot guarantee sales within a specific timeframe. We recommend treating this as a long-term passive income channel rather than an immediate revenue source.

我可以同時在其他平台販售同一件作品嗎？ Can I sell the same work on other platforms?

可以。平台只取得非獨家的數位分發授權，你可以在任何其他平台同時販售或展示同一件作品。 Yes. We hold non-exclusive digital distribution rights only. You are free to sell or display the same work on any other platform simultaneously.

作品被購買後，買家能做什麼？ What can buyers do with the work after purchase?

授權範圍視購買類型而定。個人授權允許個人列印、螢幕顯示與家庭用途；商業授權允許品牌、出版物與行銷材料使用。買家不可再次出售或轉讓授權。詳細說明請參考授權說明頁面。 The scope depends on the license type. Personal licenses allow personal printing, screen display, and home use. Commercial licenses allow use in branding, publications, and marketing materials. Buyers may not resell or transfer the license. See the Licensing page for full details.

平台對作品格式和解析度有什麼要求？ What are the format and resolution requirements?

影像類作品要求長邊最小 3000px，300 DPI，接受 PNG / JPG / TIFF / PDF。音樂類接受 MP3 / WAV（192kbps 以上）。每件作品須附低解析度預覽圖供網站顯示。 Image works require a minimum 3000px long side at 300 DPI, in PNG / JPG / TIFF / PDF format. Music accepts MP3 / WAV (192kbps or higher). Each work must include a low-res preview for website display.
